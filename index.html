About Me and My Research


Hi! I'm Xia Su, a first year PhD student in computer science @ Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science, Univeristy of Washington. My research interest lies in Human Computer Interaction, with special focus on creativity support and accessibility.

I see huge potential in the future of creativity support with the recent development of AI, while also realizing the challenges that different groups and communities are facing when trying to achieve creativity. I hope to erase these challenges with my research.

I'm supervised by Prof. Jon Froehlich at the Makeability Lab
